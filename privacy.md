<div style="padding-top:100px; text-align:center; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif;">
  <h1 style="margin-bottom: 10px;">Privacy Policy for ScopeXR</h1>
  <p style="color:#555; margin-top:0;">Effective Date: 1/25/26</p>

  <div style="max-width: 800px; margin: 0 auto; text-align: left; line-height: 1.6; font-size: 1rem; color: #333;">
    <p>ScopeXR Inc. (“ScopeXR,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) operates the ScopeXR application (the “App”) for visionOS, distributed via Apple’s public App Store. This Privacy Policy describes how information is handled when you use the App.</p>

    <hr style="margin: 24px 0; border: none; border-top: 1px solid #eee;" />

    <h2>1. Scope and Audience</h2>
    <p>ScopeXR is intended <strong>exclusively for licensed healthcare professionals</strong>, including practicing physicians. The App is <strong>not designed for children</strong> and is not directed toward individuals under the age of 13.</p>

    <hr style="margin: 24px 0; border: none; border-top: 1px solid #eee;" />

    <h2>2. Data We Do Not Collect</h2>
    <p>ScopeXR is architected to operate <strong>entirely without collecting, storing, or transmitting personal data or patient data</strong>.</p>
    <p>Specifically:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>No user accounts or authentication</li>
      <li>No names, email addresses, or personal identifiers</li>
      <li>No patient information</li>
      <li>No backend databases or cloud storage</li>
      <li>No data synchronization across devices</li>
      <li>No HealthKit or health data collection</li>
    </ul>
    <p>To the maximum extent possible, the App functions locally on the device.</p>

    <hr style="margin: 24px 0; border: none; border-top: 1px solid #eee;" />

    <h2>3. On-Device Access and Processing</h2>
    <p>The App may access certain device resources <strong>only at the user’s explicit initiation and control</strong>, and <strong>only for real-time functionality</strong>:</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Files:</strong> Local or cloud-connected files may be accessed for in-app use. File contents do not leave the device.</li>
      <li><strong>Video &amp; Network Sources:</strong> Local or network video sources, including NDI streams and HDMI/USB capture devices. All processing is local.</li>
      <li><strong>FaceTime:</strong> The App can initiate FaceTime calls using Apple’s infrastructure. Calls may include the user’s Persona and voice audio. ScopeXR does not receive or store FaceTime content.</li>
    </ul>
    <p>The App does <strong>not</strong> access photos, location data, or HealthKit.</p>

    <hr style="margin: 24px 0; border: none; border-top: 1px solid #eee;" />

    <h2>4. Analytics and Diagnostics (De-Identified)</h2>
    <p>ScopeXR uses limited third-party services for analytics and diagnostics in a <strong>de-identified and anonymized manner</strong>.</p>

    <h3>TelemetryDeck (Analytics)</h3>
    <ul>
      <li>Used for aggregated usage and performance metrics</li>
      <li>Configured in <strong>anonymous mode</strong></li>
      <li>No user accounts or persistent identifiers</li>
      <li>No user-entered content collected</li>
      <li>Technical metadata such as filenames and USB device identifiers may be logged for diagnostics only</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>Sentry (Error and Event Logging)</h3>
    <ul>
      <li>Used for crash reporting and performance monitoring</li>
      <li>May collect stack traces, breadcrumbs, and device/OS information</li>
      <li>No personal identifiers or patient data collected</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>IP Addresses</h3>
    <p>TelemetryDeck and Sentry may process IP addresses as part of standard network operations. ScopeXR does not intentionally collect, store, or associate IP addresses with individual users.</p>

    <hr style="margin: 24px 0; border: none; border-top: 1px solid #eee;" />

    <h2>5. Third-Party Libraries</h2>
    <p>The App includes the <strong>NDI</strong> library for video streaming. NDI data is processed locally or within the user’s network and is not transmitted to ScopeXR servers.</p>

    <hr style="margin: 24px 0; border: none; border-top: 1px solid #eee;" />

    <h2>6. Monetization and Advertising</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>No advertising</li>
      <li>No in-app purchases or subscriptions</li>
    </ul>

    <hr style="margin: 24px 0; border: none; border-top: 1px solid #eee;" />

    <h2>7. Regulatory Considerations</h2>
    <p>Because ScopeXR does not collect personal data:</p>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>GDPR:</strong> No processing of personal data as defined by GDPR</li>
      <li><strong>CCPA/CPRA:</strong> No collection, sale, or sharing of personal information</li>
      <li><strong>HIPAA:</strong> No creation, receipt, maintenance, or transmission of PHI</li>
    </ul>

    <hr style="margin: 24px 0; border: none; border-top: 1px solid #eee;" />

    <h2>8. Data Retention and Deletion</h2>
    <p>ScopeXR does not store personal data. De-identified diagnostic data retention is governed by third-party providers’ policies.</p>

    <hr style="margin: 24px 0; border: none; border-top: 1px solid #eee;" />

    <h2>9. Security</h2>
    <p>ScopeXR follows industry best practices emphasizing local-only processing and minimal data exposure.</p>

    <hr style="margin: 24px 0; border: none; border-top: 1px solid #eee;" />

    <h2>10. Changes to This Policy</h2>
    <p>This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Updates will be reflected by a revised effective date.</p>
